/ HOME /

Hi, my name is Jake Welch, and you have found my own personal website! A place where I do anything I want.

I created this as a place for me to have my own little corner of the web; a place where I can dump out my subconcious and hopefully connect with other like-minded people.
(and as an excuse to practice my HTML and CSS!)

The site is organized into 6 categories:

  • "Gallery", where I show my artwork, photography, and any other imagery that I produce
  • "Work bench", where you can find tools I build using code
  • "Library", where I share my favorite music, movies, TV shows, articles, historical facts, etc.
  • "Desk", an active space to leave blog posts of things on my mind, current projects I'm working on, etc.
  • "Links", a database of links to anything and everything
  • "Contacts", a place where you can reach me and say hi!

Again, I hope you enjoy your visit and find something that inspires you.
